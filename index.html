MIT Manipal Campus Guide

Welcome to MIT Manipal!

This is your simple guide to the MIT Manipal campus. Here you can find information about different places and facilities.

Campus Map

The MIT campus is located in Manipal, Karnataka. It covers a large area with many buildings, hostels, and facilities for students.

Famous Spots

End Point - A beautiful place to visit with great views

MARENA Sports Complex - Modern sports facility with world class gym and pool

Manipal Lake - Peaceful place for students to relax

Hostels

MIT has many hostels for students. There are different types of rooms available:

  • Single rooms
  • Double rooms
  • Triple rooms

Food Places

Popular places to eat on campus:

  • MIT Cafeteria - Main cafe of campus
  • KMC Food Court - Multiple food options
  • Hungry House: Known for its wrap and rolls
  • Sindhi Mess - Best for North Indian Food
  • FC2: Has the highest sitting capacity

Student Clubs

MIT has many clubs for students to join:

Technical Clubs

  • ACM Manipal - Programming club
  • IEEE - Technical activities
  • IECSE - Computer science club
  • IE Aerospace - Astronomy Club

Student Projects

  • ThrustMIT - Rocket building team
  • Formula Manipal - Race car team
  • Mars Rover Manipal - Robot building
  • Cryptonite - Hacking Team

Events

Major events at MIT Manipal:

  • Revels - Cultural festival in March
  • TechTatva - Technical festival
  • Sports Day - Annual sports competition
